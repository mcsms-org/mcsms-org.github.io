Boids Simulation

This is a Boids simulation: individual "birds" follow 3 simple rules to flock together.

The Model: We use "vector math" to calculate movement. Every bird has a direction (velocity) and tries to: (1) move away from neighbors, (2) match their speed, and (3) move toward the center of the group.

Background: This is based on Craig Reynolds' 1987 research. Download Original Paper (Reading is optional!).
